The Mist Accord

As declared by the Order of the Fog

Preamble
In times of shadow and escalation, when empires stir and silence threatens to consume the voice of reason, the Order of the Fog proclaims this Accord — not to paralyze action, but to temper it with Balance.

Tenets of the Accord

  • 1. Restraint is not weakness. It is calculation in motion.
  • 2. Retaliation must never outweigh the cost to the world.
  • 3. Measured force is the domain of guardians, not conquerors.
  • 4. Balance is preserved not through domination, but diplomacy.
  • 5. Civilian life is sacred. Even the fog shelters the innocent.
  • 6. The global commons—sea, sky, and net—must be protected.
  • 7. Dialogue must precede destruction.
  • 8. No leader shall profit by blood that was not their own.
  • 9. Unity beyond borders is the only defense against extinction.
  • 10. This Accord shall remain open. It evolves with the world.

Addendum: Clarifying Scroll (June 2025)

The Order condemns the unrelenting violence inflicted upon the civilians of Gaza. No rightful cause justifies collective punishment or displacement. The fog does not obscure this truth: land without humanity is conquest, not justice.

Division weakens the species. Unity strengthens it. We do not deny nations the right to defend, but we demand they remember their humanity.

Signed by the Fogbound, for those who seek clarity beyond chaos.
